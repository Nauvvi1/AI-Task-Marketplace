# 90-second demo script

1. Open Telegram bot.
2. Show hero card: Telegram Launch Pack.
3. Fill short brief.
4. Show order summary with deliverables and TON price.
5. Tap Pay in TON.
6. Mini App opens, wallet connects, transaction is confirmed.
7. Show order status change: Paid → In Progress.
8. Completed result arrives in Telegram.
9. Open My Orders and show history.
10. Mention one-click regenerate and TON-based payment-driven execution.
